Shuffletech

Wordpress, WooCommerce, Theme Customization, Google Analytics

Overview

Shuffletech is an e-commerce platform that offers a wide range of shuffling game-related products to customers and competitive wholesale rates to wholesalers. It incorporates efficient price filtering to help users find products that match their budget. The admin interface allows for seamless promotions, enriching customer satisfaction.

It is built using WooCommerce, and WordPress.
